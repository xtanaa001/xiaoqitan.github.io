Biography

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow in ECE@HKUST, where I did my Ph.D. with Prof. Danny H.K. Tsang from 2012 to 2017. In 2012, I received my B.Eng. (1st class honor) in communication engineering from Xi’an Jiaotong University. From Oct. 2015 to May 2016, I was a fellow in School of Engineering and Applied Science, Harvard University, hosted by Prof. Na Li. I will join University of Toronto as a postdoctoral fellow in July 2018. [Resume]

Research

I am interested in i) optimization and learning algorithms, and ii) performance modeling and stochastic analysis, current topics of interest include smart energy and transportation (e.g., smart power grids, electrified transportation networks, smart cities), and edge/fog computing systems.
