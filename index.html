Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the ECE Department at HKUST. Before joining HKUST, I received my B.E. degree from the Department of Information and Communication Engineering (first class honor), Xi’an Jiaotong University, Xi’an (the home of Terracotta Warriors and Horses), China, 2012. I am now with the research group of Computing, Communications and Energy System Optimization (C2E) in ECE department at HKUST leading by Prof. Danny H.K. Tsang. From Oct. 2015 to April 2016, I was a visiting research fellow in School of Engineering and Applied Science (SEAS), Harvard University.

Research Interests

  • Smart Cities, Energy Systems, Wireless Networking

  • Optimization, Distributed and Parallel Computing, Stochastic Analysis.
