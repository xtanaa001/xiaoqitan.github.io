Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the ECE Department at HKUST. Before joining HKUST, I received my B.Eng. degree from the Dept. of Information and Communication Engineering (first class honor), Xi’an Jiaotong University, Xi’an (the home of Terracotta Warriors and Horses), China, 2012. I am now with the research group of Computing, Communications and Energy System Optimization (C2E) in ECE department at HKUST leading by Prof. Danny H.K. Tsang. From Oct. 2015 to May 2016, I was a fellow in School of Engineering and Applied Science (SEAS), Harvard University.

Research Interests

  • Energy Systems (e.g., battery energy storage and renewable energy integration in smart power grids, power system economics)

  • Smart Cities (e.g., smart buildings and smart communities, autonomous and electric vehicles, intelligent transportation)

  • Networking and Distributed Computing (e.g., wireless networking, fog computing and edge computing, Internet of Things)

  • Optimization, Stochastic Modeling and Analysis, Distributed Algorithms, Online Learning and Online Algorithms.
